Strategic Recommendations
Execute Lane-Level Carrier Audits: Initiate targeted performance reviews for the bottom 10% of shipping lanes. Reassess existing carrier contracts and consider reallocating volume to alternative regional partners for routes exhibiting chronic delay frequency and lead-time variance.

Optimize Fulfillment Routing Logic: Utilize the newly engineered route-efficiency models to dynamically reallocate order fulfillment. If a specific factory historically struggles to meet SLAs for a given destination, automatically shift production and fulfillment to an alternative origin facility with a higher proven efficiency score.

Deploy Predictive Delivery Communication: Leverage delay magnitude algorithms to flag high-risk routes at the point of checkout. This integration empowers customer success teams to proactively manage delivery expectations or automatically upgrade shipping tiers for orders routed through known logistical bottlenecks.

Remediate Source System Infrastructure: Launch a cross-functional investigation into the root cause of the data anomalies within the upstream order management systems. Securing data hygiene at the source is a critical prerequisite for accurate operational forecasting and the deployment of real-time supply chain dashboards.
